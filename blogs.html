Mt Kenya

Mt. Kenya 3 routes run. A 96km run through Mt. Kenya’s 3 main hiking routes, summiting Lenana point three times.

Summiting 5 East African peaks

Summiting 5 East African peaks in 6 days (Rwenzori, Margherita Peak - Uganda, Mt. Muhavura - Uganda, Mt. Kenya, Mt. Meru - Tanzania and Mt. Kilimanjaro). Only taking short naps in between traveling from one mountain to the other, while at times using public transport, this is the most intensive and physically demanding challenge so far.

Manaslu

Nepal (8,163M above sea level). Together with his climbing partner, Cheruiyot Kirui, they became the first Africans to summit an 8000m peak without the use of supplemental oxygen or Sherpa support on 24th September 2023. This despite the fact that James had lost some of his fingers to frostbite on a previous expedition in April 2011.
