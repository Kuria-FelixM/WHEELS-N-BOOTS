Salmon Three Route Challenge

This grueling challenge involved ascending and descending the mountain via the Naro Moru, Chogoria, and Sirimon routes, each presenting unique technical and physical demands. The trio's accomplishment not only set a new benchmark for speed and efficiency on Mount Kenya but also underscored their exceptional training and resilience. Their achievement has been widely celebrated within the mountaineering community, inspiring both seasoned climbers and aspiring adventurers.
